About Us

Alejandra Garza

Alejandra (Alex) Garza has over 25 years of professional business and marketing development experience in the health care insurance, non-profit and public sectors, including over 10 years teaching in higher education. She has strong strategic planning, leadership development and general management business skills, as well as a proven record of managing teams with national responsibilities that deliver business results in challenging business environments.

Alex is Founder and President of AGG Consulting, Inc. where she provides strategic marketing, leadership development and business development consulting services to health care insurance companies, educational institutions, non-profits and public sector entities.

Overview

Starting in 2017, Color Us WomenTM Annual Networking event has been attended by over 200 leaders to explore and create possibilities for change that impact our communities and city. Each year, Color Us WomenTM events have featured experts on emerging issues important to African-American and Latina leaders. For the past five-years, this invitation-only has gathered Chicago’s best and brightest influencers and decision-makers representing two of the city’s most dynamic market sectors.
